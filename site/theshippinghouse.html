The Shipping Container Building

This shipping container tiny house will offer 320 square feet of living space, two bedrooms, a kitchen, and a bathroom, which will house an intern ("student caretaker"), who will live in the home year-round while managing the farm and conducting agricultural research.

Concept drawing of shipping container

Donate to the Project

On a larger scale, this project serves as a pilot for a cost-competitive blight remediation strategy - the idea being to create a livable, functional space (as opposed to demolishing a structure which is costly and results in more vacant land) that is both economically accesable to all socioeconomic background and environmentally responsible
